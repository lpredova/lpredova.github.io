About

I'm web developer from Croatia and currently I'm a full time student at Faculty of organization and informatics in Varazdin. My passion is creating stuff like websites, mobile apps or simple scripts. Also, I love boring stuff like databases and messing around a server.

I often compete on hackathons and attend development meetings, conferences because I still haven't found what I like to work one day. I simply love programming and creating stuff from ground up.

The thing I like the most is developing something just for fun or something that really sounds like a challenge, out of my comfort zone. I really enjoy working in groups where everybody does their work because it encourages me to give my best contribution to the team. Nothing makes me more confident than having knowledgeable people around who know how things are done. Being a part of a good team is AWESOME.

My biggest virtue and flaw is that I never give up till the problem is solved so sometime I overwork myself and become unproductive. Learning, and trying out new technologies is great, it's my favourite part of being a web dev.

Almost all projects I do are for non profit organizations and pet/college projects. Usually I'm a one man band, back-end/front-end web developer, DB administrator and system admin, in the extreme cases web designer.
My workflow goes from configuration of the server (Apache), making things work (PHP, Laravel, NodeJS, Python), thinking of how to handle the data ( Postgre, MySql, MongoDB) and even designing and implementing fronted front end of web applications (AngularJS, JQuery). Besides that, I know a bit of Android and Java,C, but that's totally opposite direction from web dev so I've decided to leave that to others. You can't know it all. Right?

(Currently) I have Bacc. degree but my opinion is that college sucks and degree is not (at all) as valuable as some practical coding and real project skills. I'm not too much of theoretician,I break things and then make them work even better.

So if YOU think I can help you and your team in any way free to contact me, I'm open for cooperation.
